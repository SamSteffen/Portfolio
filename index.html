About

Sam Steffen is an eager, organized, creative problem-solver with an appetite for developing and implementing new procedures to help teams work smarter, not harder. He's a strategic thinker with a talent for storytelling, strong verbal and written communication skills for customer/client-facing roles, training in data analytics (Excel, VBA, Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, API, ETL, SQL, SQLite, JavaScript, MongoDB, dbt, HTML, and more) and data visualization, an eye for the details and the deadlines.

Sam started his analytics training on-the-job doing direct service non-profit work in center city Philadelphia where he managed a mail and ID-procurement service for more than 13,000 Philadelphians experiencing housing and food-scarcity.

In 2022 he completed a certificate program in Data Analytics offered through Vanderbilt University. Since then, he has worked at Blue Cross of Idaho in the role of Data Analyst II using advanced statistical and computational methodologies and tools to deliver insights and strategic opportunities to improve the cost, quality and transparency of healthcare for clients. Sam has used MSSQL, Python and Tableau on a daily basis to analyze and deliver insights to internal teams and clients, ensuring that their PHI data remains safe. He has also played an integral role in transitioning data workflows to a secure cloud environment using Amazon Web Services and its associated products, including S3, DynamoDB, Glue, Lamba, Athena, Quicksight, and others.

Sam currently lives in Boise, Idaho but will be moving to Edmonton, Canada in July 2024 where his wife has secured employment. He is actively seeking in-person opportunities in data analytics and development in Alberta, Canada, and is open to remote opportunities throughout the provinces.
